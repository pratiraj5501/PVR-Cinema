About the movie:-
Bholaa is a 2023 Indian Hindi-language action-adventure film directed by Ajay Devgn .It is a remake of the 2019 Tamil film Kaithi. The film follows an ex-convict who battles criminals while transporting a truck full of poisoned cops to the hospital in exchange for meeting his daughter after ten years of imprisonment.
