About the movie:-
Based on Ramayana, Raghava & Lakshmana set out to rescue Janaki from demon king Lankesh. Set 7,000 years ago, Adipurush is a modern tribute to the Hindu epic Ramayana. It follows the story of prince Raghava and his brother Lakshmana’s epic journey to the island of Lanka, to rescue Raghava’s wife Janaki from the evil clutches of demon king Lankesh.
